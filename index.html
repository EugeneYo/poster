Formal or Informal?

Formal negotiation is used in many situations such as international affairs, the legal system, government, industrial disputes or domestic relationships. While informal negotiation is used in situation where no legal documents are involved eg, buying and selling at the wet market.


Negotiation skills can be learned and applied in a wide range of activities. Having the required negotiation skills can be of great benefit in resolving any differences that arise between the two parties.
